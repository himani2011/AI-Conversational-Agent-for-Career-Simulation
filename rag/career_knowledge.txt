Career Coaching Knowledge Base

Resume Tips:
A strong resume should be one page long for students and early professionals.
Use action verbs like built, developed, implemented, designed.
Focus on measurable achievements instead of responsibilities.
Example: "Improved model accuracy by 18%" instead of "Worked on ML model".

Interview Preparation:
Behavioral interviews often use the STAR method:
Situation – describe the context
Task – describe your responsibility
Action – explain what you did
Result – explain the outcome with numbers if possible.

Technical Interview Tips:
Always clarify the problem before coding.
Think out loud while solving problems.
Start with brute force, then optimize.

Career Advice for Software Engineers:
Build projects and publish them on GitHub.
Focus on data structures and algorithms.
Practice system design basics.

Career Advice for Data Scientists:
Strong statistics and probability knowledge is required.
Learn Python libraries: pandas, numpy, scikit-learn.
Build end-to-end ML projects.

Career Advice for AI Engineers:
Understand deep learning and neural networks.
Learn about LLMs, transformers and RAG systems.
Work with APIs and deployment.

Networking Advice:
Most jobs come from networking.
Optimize your LinkedIn profile.
Attend meetups and career fairs.

Common Career Mistakes:
Applying without tailoring resume.
Not preparing for behavioral interviews.
Not having real projects to show.
